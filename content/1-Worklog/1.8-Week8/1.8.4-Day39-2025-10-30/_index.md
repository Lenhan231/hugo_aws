# **Pragmatics – Intended Meaning in Context**

**Pragmatics** studies meaning that comes from **context, speaker intention, and real-world knowledge**.

## It Covers:
- **Implicature:** Hidden or implied meaning beyond literal words
- **Deixis:** Context-dependent references (this/that/here/you…)
- **Speech acts:** Promises, requests, apologies, commands
- **Politeness, formality, and sarcasm:** Tone and intention behind words

## NLP Relevance:
- **Dialogue systems:** Understanding user intent in conversations
- **Chatbots:** Responding appropriately to context
- **Sentiment irony detection:** Understanding sarcasm and hidden feelings
- **Contextual language models:** BERT, GPT, and transformers that understand context

### Search Engines
Your daily searches are facilitated by NLP for query understanding and ranking.
